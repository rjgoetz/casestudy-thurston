In The Kitchen

Chef Nathan Thurston

Chef and Owner Nathan Thurston has been cooking in the Carolinas for over twenty-three years. Fifteen of these years have bee in beautiful Charleston, SC where he commanded the kitchens of The Ocean Room, Jasmine Porch, The Art Institue of Charleston, Stars Grill Room, and the five star & five diamond rated Sanctuary Hotel at Kiawah Island Golf Resort

Chef Thurston has built a diverse and expansive network during this exciting hospitality journey. This network and our culinary skill set will be strategically leveraged to assist you with your catering & hospitality needs.
